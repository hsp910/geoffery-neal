I started making giant bubble mix as a way to entertain kids at a school potluck back in 2004. Since then I have been providing giant bubbles at events all over the place, including Shakori Hills Grassroots Music Festival in Chatham County, Saturdays at Saxapahaw, Clydefest in Bynum, Slice of Summer in Graham, Active City Streets in Burlington, WUNC 40th Anniversary Celebration in Cary, NC Botanical Garden in Chapel Hill and Pepperfest in Pittsboro.
I can provide bubble mix and wands for small and large events. I bring everything you need for fun.
